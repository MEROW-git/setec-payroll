# Human-Resource-Management

 Siegecode HRM is designed to be a comprehensive and modern Human Resource Management system specifically tailored for modern businesses. Its primary purpose is to streamline workforce management, automate payroll processes, and provide data-driven insights into employee performance and organizational health.

## [Explore HRM System](https://hrm-five-nu.vercel.app/)

Here are the key features of the Siegecode HRM system:
### 1. Centralized Employee Management
Employee Profiles: Detailed tracking of personal information, employment history, documents, and roles.
Directory: A searchable list of all employees across different departments.
Role-Based Access: Specialized views for Admins (HR Directors) and Employees.
### 2. Department & Hierarchy Organization
Department Management: Organize employees into functional groups like Sales, Inventory, Design, and HR.
Strategic Structure: Visualizing the company structure for better communication.
### 3. Time & Attendance Tracking
Daily Attendance: Digital logs for clock-in/out times.
Shift Management: Planning and assigning shifts to ensure optimal staffing at all times.
Real-time Status: Tracking who is currently active, on break, or absent.
### 4. Leave & Absence Management
Leave Requests: Digital workflow for employees to apply for vacation, sick leave, or personal time.
Approval System: HR can review, approve, or reject leave requests with a clear audit trail.
Balance Tracking: Automatic updates of remaining leave balances.
### 5. Payroll & Compensation
Automated Calculations: Streamlining salary processing based on attendance and bonuses.
Payslip Generation: Digital payslips accessible to employees.
### 6. Performance & KPI Tracking
Reviews: Structured performance evaluations.
KPI Tracking: Monitoring key performance indicators to identify top talent and areas for growth.
### 7. Operational Tools
Dashboard: Real-time overview of total employees, active leaves, upcoming events, and quick stats.
Notification System: Integrated sidebar for alerts regarding approvals, announcements, and tasks.
Event Scheduling: A calendar view for company-wide events and deadlines.
### 8. Security & User Experience
Secure Authentication: Secure login portal for different user roles.
Responsive Design: A clean, modern interface that works across devices, featuring smooth animations for a polished feel.
